Instantly create an elegant video presentation website.

Are you sending your users to YouTube to watch your videos? Are you struggling to find a way to get your video content onto your own site? Do you wish you could bring together all the videos about your community or product in one place? Do you ever wish you had your own Hulu?

Need a Solution? Contact Us >>

Would the TED Conference ever send people to YouTube to see their videos? Of course not. For an organization like TED or Hulu, their own brand is simply too important for them to send their users over to YouTube. Instead, they built their own video presentation sites. For your organization, presenting your videos well is just as important-- but designing and coding your own site is very expensive. That's where we come in: Miro Community gives you a fast and simple way to create a beautiful video presentation site on your own website.

What is Miro Community? Miro Community is a web service that gives your organization a beautiful, easy customized and branded video presentation website on your own domain. If your organization creates video or if people in your community do, Miro Community lets you bring it all together to reach more viewers and strengthen your relationships and your brand. It's the missing link in your video plans.

What can Miro Community do for your organization?

"The product we build has an enthusiastic community and lots of users are making videos about it. We also produce how-to videos from time to time. We'd like bring our users together on a homebase for video on our website."

"Our college is publishing courseware on iTunes U, but we aren't reaching people on the web."
